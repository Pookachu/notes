#### Product Rule
**Formula:**
$$
x^m \cdot x^n = x^m+n
$$
	**Example:**
$$
x^2 \cdot x^3 = x^5
$$
#### Quotient Rule
Formula:
$$
\frac{x^m}{x^n} = x^{m-n}
$$
Example:
$$
\frac{x^6}{x^2} = x^4
$$
#### Power Rule
Formula: 
$$
(x^m)^n = x^{m \cdot n}
$$
Example:
$$
(x^3)^2 = x^6
$$
#### Zero Exponent
Formula
$$
x^0 = 1
$$
Example:
$$
12345^0 = 1
$$
#### Negative Exponent
Formula:

$$
x^{-n} = \frac{1}{x^n}
$$
Example:
$$
	x^{-3} = \frac{1}{x^3}
$$
